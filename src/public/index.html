What are VIP Tokens?

VIP Tokens are the digital currency of Kea Valley Estates, designed to unlock a world of luxury services across the island. Each token represents EUR 1 equivalent that can be spent on premium experiences, from villa rentals to fine dining, wellness treatments to exclusive island excursions.

As a token holder, you gain access to member-only discounts, seasonal rewards, and priority booking for the most sought-after experiences on Kea Island. The more tokens you hold, the greater your benefits.
