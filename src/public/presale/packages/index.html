The Kea Valley Private Members Club

Invitation-Only Membership

The Kea Valley Membership Club ('KV Club') is an invitation-only membership club situated on the island of Kea, Greece, which operates solely for the benefit of its members—leading business people and other lovers of Kea and Greece.

group 2,500 registered members as of Dec 2025

visibility Membership Vision

The vision of the Club is to provide an environment where its members can enjoy their lives, their families, their sport, the beauty of Kea, and their hobbies while developing Kea. Kea Valley offers world-class levels of private security.

This is a community whose members have their own yachts and private jets, and want to own a private villa on Kea with their own private hangar close by and a private marina.
