The Concept

A Vision of Elegance

Set along the stunning coastline of Kea-Kythnos in Greece, this 460 sq.m. luxury residence rests on a generous 4,000 sq.m. plot, offering breathtaking sea views and unforgettable Mediterranean sunsets.

Designed across two levels, the villa blends indoor elegance with the natural beauty of its surroundings. The main level features a spacious living and dining area with fireplace, a high-standard open-plan kitchen, and wide panoramic openings that lead seamlessly to the infinity pool and outdoor terraces. An en-suite bedroom and guest W.C. complete this floor.

The lower pool level hosts three master bedrooms, a guest house, an entertainment area, and additional service quarters, creating a private sanctuary for residents and guests alike.
